Who are we?

We provide facilities to nurture new ideas and help develop inquisitive perspectives in youths. We provide opportunities for startUps to meet potential investors and funders while providing a platform for business networking.

Our Impacts

We have nurtured several startups in the country by providing them with the needs to succeed in this economy. We have also trained youths on creative skills, problem identification and solving and how to sell their ideas through seminars, workshops and hackathons.

StartUps

As a startup enabler we help to facilitate growth of the startup ecosystem through means of investment, mentorship, connections and market access.
